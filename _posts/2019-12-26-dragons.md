## Lindorm

_Lindwyrm, Norse dragons._

Snake-like with two legs.

## Chinese Dragon

_Long dragons, Eastern Asian Dragon._

Snake-like with 4 legs.

## Wyvern

Two legs and a pair of wings.

## European Dragon

Four legs and a pair of wings.
